About GSS

Gudheniya Storage Solution (GSS) is India’s premier hub for industrial efficiency, founded in 2023. Based in Agra, we empower businesses nationwide with intelligent storage layouts and robust racking systems designed to maximize space and productivity.

By integrating high-tensile steel with our advanced 7-tank powder coating technology, we deliver certified load-bearing solutions that guarantee a rust-free life and superior durability in the most demanding warehouse environments.
